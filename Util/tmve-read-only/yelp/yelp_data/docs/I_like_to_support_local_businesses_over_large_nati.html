I like to support local businesses over large national chains. I used to study a lot at the Xtreme Bean. But I can't go there anymore. Love their coffee and their prices. Hate their politics. When I learned that they were at the pro-SB 1070 rally and that they were tea party supporters, I made the choice to no longer go there. I doubt the loss of one customer will make a difference in their business. But at least it gives me a little more control over how my dollar is ultimately spent.
