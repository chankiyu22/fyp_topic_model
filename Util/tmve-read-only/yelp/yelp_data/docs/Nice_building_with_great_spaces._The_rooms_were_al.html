Nice building with great spaces. The rooms were always comfortable to be in (in some center the AC is on blast!) and everything looked new. The bathrooms were nice, clean, and well kept, even during rush hours for our conference. the layout was good for a large building without being too confusing. The locationis close a few hotels and a shopping center, which has a few good food places.



My notes of improvement would start with the wifi, which was a problem throughout our four day conference. Please work with your hosts to provide better wifi services. The second note of improvement were the doors I each room I went to were very loud when persons were leaving and entering. Maybe installing a quiet closer for the doors would be nice. I also did not like the food here, but hey, it's a convention center.



Overall, a great convention center that is about to accondate large conferences.
