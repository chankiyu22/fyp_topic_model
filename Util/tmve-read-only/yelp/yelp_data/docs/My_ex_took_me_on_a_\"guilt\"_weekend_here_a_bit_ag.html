My ex took me on a "guilt" weekend here a bit ago. He got me a massage, body wrap, the works!

The room was really cozy and the bed was so comfortable I didn't want to get up...but I had that darn massage to get to!

We had dinner and the staff was extremely friendly.

What really "made" the weekend, though, was the spa!

First, I had a deep tissue massage. Divine. Not bad for a hotel spa, you know?

Second, I had a red wine body wrap. Since I can't drink it, might as well soak in it, right? She slathered on the red wine/aloe mixture. It felt sooooooooooooo good...but nothing compared to when it was all done and she covered me up for a half hour and put heavy blankets on me. I fell asleep!

The cool part was when she unwrapped me. I looked like the mom & daughter from Poltergeist when they were covered in jelly but it really looked like afterbirth.

There's a private shower in the room to get all the "gunk" off.

I was so relaxed!

Parking was a bit complicated and why I took away one star. Meh.
