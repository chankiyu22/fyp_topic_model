It feels kind of wrong giving Chipotle the same amount of stars that I gave Posh.



But its so delicious! Chipotle is a great, quick meal without the heavy greasy feeling of fast food. Their black beans, rice and tortillas are all vegetarian-friendly and their burritos can easily be made vegan without the sour cream and cheese. It is kind of a crap shoot how large your burrito will be - sometimes you get a lot, sometimes you get a little...but the flavor is always consistent.



This location gets super crowded between 11:30-1:30 with lines that can stretch out the door. I always order online - it is really easy and you get to walk right in and pick it up without waiting in that awful line!
