Looking for northern Italian fare, Un Bacio at Gainy Ranch is inviting the moment you enter their doors. The decor is impressive with over sized urns filled with rich flowers. The long bar area, where a vocalist hits every appropriate note accommodates diners with cocktail tables to dine at as well. I choose to have dinner in the main room which accommodates large groups as well as small intimate tables.



As for the service, the staff are friendly and very attentive.



Our choice of salads were the seafood and caesar both very tasty. The bolognese sauce was flavorful and a good choice as well although the meatballs were not up to my standards. A bit pasty and hard. As for the dessert, the flour less chocolate cake was scrumptious and perfect to share.



For a neighborhood restaurant a definite go to spot.
