I decided to write a review on this particular Oregano's because of the great service I was given tonight.



So I was a bad customer; I didn't fully read the menu on one of the salads when it explicitly stated that it had chicken. I had it in my head that our waiter (Bronson, you were awesome!) had asked me if I wanted to add chicken for $2.00 and I declined. When the salad came out with chicken I called him back and told him that I wanted the one without, and I was basically told, in the most polite way imaginable, to read the f*cking menu again, especially the part where it says chicken. So I felt like a dumb ass, but they were great and let me go on thinking that it was their fault and they rushed another salad out to me in literally two minutes.



In short, Oregano's always has the best service and nicest staff working there. Doesn't hurt that the food is great, too, but you guys definitely went above and beyond tonight. Thank you!
