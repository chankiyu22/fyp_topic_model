I went to this Trader Joe's on the fourth of July and it was busier than I expected.



Overall, I like all TJ because the quality and affordability!



This TJ is no different from any other. It is clean, employees are welcoming and nice. They all stock the same items.



The items I always love to get from TJ are:



+Kale (affordable, pre-washed and delicious and only $1.99!!, such a healthy food!)

+Cookie butter (Oh my... Have you guys tried it? I feel like I died and went to heaven buying thing! So unique to TJ!! )

+Crumpets (Cookie butter + crumpets are delicious and both are reasonably priced)

+Garbanzo beans - cheap and good, goes great with salad or make hummus with it!

+Grape tomatoes - I just love to buy tomatoes from TJ!

+Olive oil (best deal in town, seriously!)

+Balsamic vinegar

+Chocolate covered pretzels - BEST THING EVER! The pinch of salt brings out the flavor





There's so many snacks and frozen foods I just love from there... the list might go on and on....



+I used to think TJ was like whole foods, fancy and overpriced but when you stop and look at the prices and quality, a lot of the snacks and frozen foods and some of the vegetables are reasonably price and worth it!
