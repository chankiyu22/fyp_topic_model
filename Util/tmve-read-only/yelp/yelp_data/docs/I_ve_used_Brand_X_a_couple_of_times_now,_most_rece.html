I've used Brand X a couple of times now, most recently for a group Halloween costume. They are very patient, however, YOU must provide the creativity. In my experience, it works best if you come with your own design -- not just as an idea in your head but executed artwork. I like that they keep the artwork on file, in case you want to reprint (or print additional). The quality both of the shirts and the actual printing are very good and they're quick!
