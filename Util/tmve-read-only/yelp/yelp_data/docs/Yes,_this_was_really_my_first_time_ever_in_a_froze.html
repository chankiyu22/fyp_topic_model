Yes, this was really my first time ever in a frozen yogurt shop. I have heard about them for years, but just never went in until I arrived on my first night in this city. Too exhausted to chew, yogurt sounded great. I felt like I walked into heaven upon entering the door! Very white and clean. Enormous buffet of gigantic toppings-- fresh ripe berries, toasted coconut, kiwi fruit!!! Made me so happy to be able to have this heavenly treat!!! Staff was friendly and offered a sample, but I knew mango was for me without trying it. Signed up for a loyalty card, and since this place is just a short hop from me, I will go back. Full size cookies, brownies, moon pies, and more to put on the bottom! And the neatest thing was the tiny cream puffs! Might not want to load up on heavier toppings as it is sold by weight, but I had fun being allowed to create my own treat.
