If you like hot waitresses, gold diggers, and cougars... run to Narcisse! If not, feel free to meander in when you're out on the town in the North Dale. Besides the interesting mix of barbies and douche bags, I think I would have liked the venue much more if I didn't go with a big group. We had been put on the list in advance and supposedly had "VIP" status. The VIP status basically means it takes you 30 minutes to get in the club because you have to wait for the bouncer to get you into the secret back entrance, then wait to get taken up the elevator, then wait in a secret teeny tiny room with a bunch of people for the host to come get you. There was a lot of confusion with who was serving our group/table, so it made it less enjoyable. The d\u00e9cor was sexy but presented with an upscale "club" vibe, and the layout was fairly open. The two main downfalls to Narcisse are they play dance music, but have no dance floor, and the only outdoor patio is the smoking section.
