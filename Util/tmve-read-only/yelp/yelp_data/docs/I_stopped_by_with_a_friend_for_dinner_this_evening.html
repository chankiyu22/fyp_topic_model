I stopped by with a friend for dinner this evening not knowing what to expect because my last couple indian restaurant experiences were not positive. As you approach the building confidence is inspired with a tidy entrance and colorful landscaping. The menu offered many options and while we were waiting the glasses were always full. When the food arrived it all came together brilliantly. Both dishes were excellent and I will absolutely be back.



Oh, one more note. We didn't have a single waiter, we had a team throughout our visit with everyone working in sync to get us what ever was needed! The manager stopped by a couple times and it was clear that he took pride in his restaurant.
