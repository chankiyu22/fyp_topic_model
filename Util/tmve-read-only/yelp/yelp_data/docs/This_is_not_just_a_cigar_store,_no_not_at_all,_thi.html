This is not just a cigar store, no not at all, this is a social event.



The staff at Casa Fuma is very knowledgeable, if you know little to nothing about cigars the staff will walk through their humidor with you to find something that they are sure you will like, they do not try to push off old inventory or sell a high end cigar to someone who does not know better, I have seen them on multiple occasions spend a lot of time with someone to find a cigar that the customer would enjoy. If you are a seasoned cigar smoker and are looking for something different or new, they can also help you. They have a vast selection from flavored cigars all the way up to super premium cigars.



Casa Fuma is not somewhere you go to buy a few cigars and leave (although you can do that) more often than not I see groups come in together buy a few cigars and spend the evening smoking and watching a football game or movie on one of their multiple flat screen t.v.s. This is a very social place, it can be very quite and intimate, and crowded and rowdy (when they have live bands playing).



Casa Fuma offers lock boxes at the store so you can store cigars (and "drinks") there at the store, the lockers are humidified so that your cigars will be well preserved until your next visit. I only wish that Casa Fuma had a liquor license (not that this is stopping the customers from having a good time).



Cigars are not all, they also offer supplies for pipe smoking, they carry cloves as well as hookah's and the needed supplies.



This is a great place a must see if this kind of thing is what you enjoy!
